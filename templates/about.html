About Us

The G1Bbon benchmark was developed by Gonçalo Guiomar, Elia Torre, and Mario Giulianelli at University of Zurich and ETH Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Valerio Mante

The Temporal Reasoning Task is a research platform designed to investigate how different agents - both human and artificial - identify and process temporal patterns. Through a series of interactive tasks, we study the cognitive processes involved in detecting statistical biases that emerge over time.
